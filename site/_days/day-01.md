## Evening

<div class="activity-block">
<div class="activity-time">🌙 Evening — Sensoji Temple</div>

<div class="activity-card">
<h3>Sensoji Temple</h3>
<p>Tokyo's oldest temple is stunning in the evening — fewer crowds and beautifully lit up. Walk through the Thunder Gate (Kaminarimon), browse Nakamise shopping street, and take in the temple grounds.</p>
<p><strong>Why evening?</strong> Less crowded, cooler temperatures, and the lanterns are gorgeous after dark. The shops on Nakamise close around 5-6 PM, but the temple grounds are open and atmospheric.</p>
<div class="activity-meta">
<a href="https://maps.google.com/?q=Sensoji+Temple+Asakusa">📍 Get Directions</a>
<span>⏱️ 45-60 min</span>
</div>

## Tips for Day 1

- **Don't fight jet lag too hard** — Stay up until a reasonable local bedtime (8-9 PM is fine)
- **Hydrate** — Long flights are dehydrating
- **eSIM** — Should be activated before landing (set up at home before departure)
- **IC Card** — Get a Suica or Pasmo card at the airport for trains
