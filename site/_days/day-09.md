## Morning

<div class="activity-block">
<div class="activity-time">🌅 Morning</div>

<div class="activity-card">
<h3>Pokemon Center OSAKA DX</h3>
<p>The big one! Located in Daimaru Shinsaibashi department store. Start here for official merchandise and current sealed products.</p>
<p><strong>What to look for:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Japan-exclusive merchandise</li>
<li>Current booster boxes and packs</li>
<li>Plushies and accessories</li>
<li>Osaka-exclusive items (if any)</li>
</ul>
<div class="activity-meta">
<a href="https://maps.google.com/?q=Pokemon+Center+Osaka+DX">📍 Get Directions</a>
<span>⏱️ 1-2 hours</span>
</div>
</div>

<div class="activity-card">
<h3>Daimaru Shinsaibashi + Shinsaibashi-suji</h3>
<p>You're already in the department store — take advantage of the AC. Browse the floors, then spill out onto the covered Shinsaibashi-suji shopping arcade right outside, which runs all the way toward Dotonbori.</p>
<p><strong>Lunch:</strong> Plenty of options in the depachika (basement food hall) or along the arcade before the afternoon card hunt.</p>
<div class="activity-meta">
<a href="https://maps.google.com/?q=Shinsaibashi+suji+Shopping+Street">📍 Shinsaibashi-suji</a>
</div>

## Afternoon

<div class="activity-block">
<div class="activity-time">☀️ Afternoon — CARD HUNTING TIME</div>

<div class="activity-card">
<h3>Nipponbashi / Den-Den Town Card Shop Walk</h3>
<p>This is the main event for singles! You did recon yesterday — now it's time to buy. Plan to visit 3-5 shops minimum.</p>
<p><strong>Strategy:</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>You already know where things are from yesterday's recon</li>
<li>Hit the shops with the best prices on your target cards first</li>
<li>Compare across shops for anything new</li>
<li>Don't hesitate — this is your main card hunting day</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Shops to hit:</strong> Card Labo, Hobby Station, Mandarake Namba (bulk/vintage), Book-Off (discounted sealed), various smaller shops</p>
<p><strong>Business tip:</strong> Ask for tax-free on purchases over ¥5,000 per store. Have passport ready. Consolidate purchases at one store when possible.</p>
<div class="activity-meta">
<a href="https://maps.google.com/?q=Nipponbashi+Osaka">📍 Nipponbashi Area</a>
<span>⏱️ 2-3 hours</span>
</div>
</div>

</div>
