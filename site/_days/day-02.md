## Afternoon

<div class="activity-block">
<div class="activity-time">☀️ Afternoon — Riverside + AC Escapes</div>

<div class="activity-card">
<h3>Sumida River Walk</h3>
<p>Stroll along the Sumida River toward Azumabashi Bridge for a classic view of Tokyo Skytree and the Asahi building across the water. Sumida Park is pleasant and shaded in spots.</p>
<p><strong>Heat option:</strong> Hop a short Tobu train or walk across to Tokyo Skytree Town (Solamachi) — a big AC mall under the tower with shops, an aquarium, and a Pokemon Center.</p>
<div class="activity-meta">
<a href="https://maps.google.com/?q=Sumida+Park+Asakusa">📍 Sumida Park</a>
</div>
</div>

<div class="activity-card">
<h3>Tokyo Skytree (optional)</h3>
<p>If anyone has the energy, ride up Tokyo Skytree for sweeping city views, or just enjoy Solamachi below. Tokyo Skytree Town also has the Sumida Aquarium and the Pokemon Center Skytree Town — an easy AC win.</p>
<div class="activity-meta">
<a href="https://maps.google.com/?q=Tokyo+Skytree">📍 Tokyo Skytree</a>
<span>⏱️ flexible</span>
</div>
