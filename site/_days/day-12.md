## Heat & Weather

<div class="activity-block">
<div class="activity-time">🌡️ Be Ready</div>

<div class="activity-card">
<h3>Dress for the Lakes</h3>
<p>It's cooler at the lakes than in central Tokyo, but still a hot July day with lots of sun exposure at open viewpoints.</p>
<ul>
<li>Sunscreen, hat, water bottle</li>
<li>Comfortable shoes (Chureito Pagoda steps)</li>
<li>A light layer for AC on the bus / breezy lakeshore</li>
<li>Camera / phone fully charged</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="alert alert-info">
<p class="alert-title">If Mt. Fuji is hidden</p>
<p>Summer is the haziest season and the peak is often shy — it happens, and it's nobody's fault. The lakes, pagoda, and countryside still make a beautiful day. Check a "Mt Fuji Kawaguchiko live camera" the night before to set expectations.</p>
</div>
