## Getting to Arashiyama

From central Kyoto, several options:
- **JR Sagano Line** from Kyoto Station to Saga-Arashiyama (~15 min)
- **Hankyu Line** to Arashiyama Station
- **Keifuku Randen** tram line (scenic, old-school)
