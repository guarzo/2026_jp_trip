## Convenience Store (Konbini) Guide

Don't sleep on konbini food — it's legitimately great!

### Best Items to Try

**7-Eleven:**
- Onigiri (rice balls)
- Egg salad sandwiches
- Fried chicken (famichiki-style)
- Premium desserts

**FamilyMart:**
- Famichiki (fried chicken)
- Pasta dishes
- Sweets

**Lawson:**
- Karaage-kun (fried chicken nuggets)
- Premium roll cake
- Oden (winter comfort food)

### Pro Tips
- Hot food section near register
- Microwave available for bento
- Look for "limited edition" seasonal items
- Late-night discounts on expiring items

## Eating Etiquette Reminders

- **No tipping** — Really, don't
- **Itadakimasu** before eating (like "bon appetit")
- **Gochisousama** after eating (thanks for the meal)
- **Slurping noodles** is polite — shows you enjoy it!
- **Don't walk and eat** — Find a spot to stand/sit
- **Chopstick rules:** Never stick upright in rice, don't pass food chopstick-to-chopstick
- **Oshibori** (wet towel) is for hands only, not face
