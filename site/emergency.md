## Allergy Cards (Japanese)

If anyone has food allergies, prepare cards in Japanese. Common allergies:

| Allergy | Japanese |
|---------|----------|
| Peanuts | ピーナッツアレルギー |
| Tree nuts | ナッツアレルギー |
| Shellfish | 甲殻類アレルギー |
| Fish | 魚アレルギー |
| Eggs | 卵アレルギー |
| Milk/Dairy | 乳製品アレルギー |
| Wheat/Gluten | 小麦アレルギー |
| Soy | 大豆アレルギー |

**Phrase:** "I have a ___ allergy" = ___アレルギーがあります (___arerugii ga arimasu)

## Lost Item Contacts

Japan has excellent lost-and-found. If you lose something:

### Trains
- **JR East Lost & Found:** 03-3231-1880
- **Tokyo Metro:** 03-3834-5577
- Go to the station master's office

### Taxis
- Keep your receipt! It has the taxi company's number
- Or call the taxi association

### General
- **Koban (Police Box):** Found items often go here
- Check back at the location — staff hold items

## WiFi Backup

If your portable WiFi fails:

1. **Convenience stores** — Free WiFi (7-Eleven, FamilyMart, Lawson)
2. **Train stations** — JR stations have free WiFi
3. **Starbucks** — Free WiFi
4. **Buy a SIM** — Electronics stores, airport

## Quick Reference

- **Japan country code:** +81
- **Voltage:** 100V (US plugs work, but some devices charge slower)
- **Tipping:** Not expected (can be offensive)
- **Cash:** Still king in many places — always carry some
- **Convenience stores:** ATMs that take foreign cards (7-Eleven, Lawson)
